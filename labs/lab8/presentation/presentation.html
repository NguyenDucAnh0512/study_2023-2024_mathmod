Задание

\[ \frac{dM_1}{d\theta}=M_1-\frac{b}{c_1}M_1 M_2-\frac{a_1}{c_1}M^2_1 \]

\[ \frac{dM_2}{d\theta}=\frac{c_2}{c_1}M_2-\frac{b}{c_1}M_1 M_2-\frac{a_2}{c_1}M^2_2 \]

function dx=syst(t, x)
